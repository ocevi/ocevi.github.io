Playas.ieo.es

Marine meteorology at the Cantabrian and Atlantic beaches of the Iberian Peninsula.

CTD Check

Quality control, metadating and standardization of ocean vertical profiles (CTD) under SeaDataNet criteria.

Upwelling index

Upwelling index computations off Northwest Iberia.

CO2 uncertainties

Interactive visualization of the uncertainties associated to sea-air CO2 flux estimations in the Bay of Biscay.

Oil spill coastal impact

Interactive visualization of the coastal impact of an eventual oil spill coming from the Finisterre maritime traffic corridor.

Marine litter viewer

Marine litter viewer (beach, floating and bottom) developed in the frame of the CleanAtlantic project.

Twilitter

Interactive visualization of tweets about marine pollution by plastics.

Projects

with participation of members of the team

CLEANATLANTIC

Tackling Marine Litter in the Atlantic Area

CleanAtlantic aims to protect biodiversity and ecosystem services in the Atlantic Area by improving capabilities to monitor, prevent and remove (macro) marine litter. The project will also contribute to raise awareness and change attitudes among stakeholders and to improve marine litter managing systems.

Visit the web project

ANDROMEDA

A JPI Oceans Microplastics Research Project

ANDROMEDA brings together 15 international partners across Europe and Canada in an expert consortium to tackle challenges in characterising & quantifying microplastics.

Visit the web project

COMET

IEO Metadata catalogue

Configuration and maintenance of the metadata catalogue of the Insitituto Español de Oceanografía

Visit the web project

DIVERSIMAR

Observation network of marine and fisheries biodiversity of Galicia and Cantabrian

DIVERSIMAR is a tool to obtain information and monitor the marine and fishing biodiversity of Galicia and the Cantabrian Sea.

Visit the web project
